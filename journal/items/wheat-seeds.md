# Wheat Seeds

Drops from wheat harvests. The right-click harvest auto-replants without consuming inventory seeds, so no reserve is held — the bot keeps 0 on hand and deposits all seeds into the [[../chests/house-kitchen-chest|kitchen chest]] (see [[../procedures/deposit-seeds]]). Surplus seeds also feed `craftPlantBalls` in the sustain loop.

## Keep-on-hand
- **0** (per [[../procedures/deposit-seeds]] policy — right-click harvest auto-replants, so no reserve is needed)
